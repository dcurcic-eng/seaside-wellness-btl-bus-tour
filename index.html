On the BTL Bus — Consultations, Demos & Mini Sessions

BTL Exomind

The BTL Exomind is a revolutionary non-invasive brain stimulation device that uses Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) technology. By delivering focused magnetic pulses to specific areas of the brain, Exomind helps improve mental wellness, mood, and cognitive function — all without medication or surgery.

This FDA-cleared technology is designed for patients seeking relief from depression, anxiety, and other mental health challenges. At the event, you'll be able to speak directly with our providers, see the device up close, and — if you'd like — try a quick, comfortable mini session. The treatment is painless, requires no anesthesia, and sessions typically last around 20 minutes.

  • Talk to our experts and get a personal consultation
  • FDA-cleared for mental wellness applications
  • No medication, no surgery, no downtime
  • Quick, comfortable 20-minute sessions
  • Targeted therapy for depression & anxiety relief

Body Contouring & Skin Tightening

BTL Emtone

BTL Emtone is the first and only device that simultaneously delivers both thermal energy and mechanical targeted pressure energy to treat cellulite at its source. By addressing all five contributing factors of cellulite — enlarged fat chambers, damaged collagen fibers, loss of skin elasticity, poor blood flow, and metabolic waste buildup — Emtone delivers superior results compared to any single-energy device.

Treatments are comfortable and feel similar to a hot stone massage combined with intense vibrations. There is zero downtime, allowing patients to return to their normal activities immediately. Most patients see noticeable improvement after just four sessions.

  • Dual-energy cellulite reduction technology
  • Targets all 5 root causes of cellulite
  • Comfortable treatment — like a hot stone massage
  • Zero downtime, return to activities immediately
  • Visible results in as few as 4 sessions
